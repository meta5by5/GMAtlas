This is the ready-to-paste body text for a new Guide document titled
"5PFH Campaign Turn Sequence" (docs/adr/0018-lightweight-rich-text.md).

Campaign data (including Guide documents) lives in each GM's own browser
storage, not this repo — so this file is the durable, versioned copy of
that content. To use it: open the Guide drawer, click "+ Root doc", title
it "5PFH Campaign Turn Sequence", paste everything below the line into the
editor, then click elsewhere to save. The @[...] references below become
real clickable, correctly-paged links into the Reference Library the
moment they're saved (the same @mention syntax every other field in this
app already uses) — nothing further to do.

Every page reference from the original pasted outline is preserved:
"Core" resolves to 5PFH Five Parsecs From Home v3, "CBH" resolves to 5PFH
3e Compendium Includes Bug Hunt, and a bare page number (no book prefix)
resolves to Hostile setting, per the user's explicit rule. The Mission
Steps' numbering is corrected to a real 1-8 sequence (the source's "1., 1.,
1., ..." was a Markdown auto-numbering artifact, not intentional); its
lettered post-battle sub-steps (a-q) and deployment sub-considerations are
pulled into their own labeled lists directly below, since this app's
lightweight list renderer supports one flat list level, not true nesting —
see the ADR's Alternatives Considered for why. A few duplicated/garbled
trailing lines in the original source (after post-battle step "q") read as
a copy-paste artifact, not real content, and are dropped.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

**Daily Life Steps**
- Update status (injuries, cargo, heat, rivals, favors, reputation).
- Assign/resolve crew tasks (@[Core p.77|5PFH Five Parsecs From Home v3#77]).
- Determine job offers (@[Core p.83|5PFH Five Parsecs From Home v3#83]).
- Check for Rivals (@[Core p.85|5PFH Five Parsecs From Home v3#85]).
- Select your Job (Patron, Rival, Quest, Salvage, etc.).
- Resolve Rumors (@[Core p.85|5PFH Five Parsecs From Home v3#85]).
- Resolve Mission Steps or Merchant Mission Steps.
- Story Event/Track if turn without one (@[Core p.66|5PFH Five Parsecs From Home v3#66], @[Core p.153|5PFH Five Parsecs From Home v3#153]).

**Mission Steps**
1. Determine the objective (may use Expanded Missions, @[p.74|Hostile setting#74])
2. Mission Selection (Salvage Jobs)
3. Faction Involvement* (unknown during selection; could be a surprise) (@[CBH p.110|5PFH 3e Compendium Includes Bug Hunt#110])
4. Check for Connections (@[p.164|Hostile setting#164], or Expanded Connection @[CBH p.80|5PFH 3e Compendium Includes Bug Hunt#80] – Opportunity mission only)
5. Assign equipment (@[p.85|Hostile setting#85])
6. Deployment (@[pp.88-94|Hostile setting#88]) — see Deployment considerations below
7. Battle (go to tabletop)
8. Resolve Tabletop Post-battle activities — see Post-battle activities below

**Deployment considerations** (step 6)
- Check deployment conditions (not Salvage)
- Determine notable sights (not Salvage)
- Determine the enemy (@[p.92|Hostile setting#92])
- Set up the battlefield (@[p.108|Hostile setting#108]; Terrain Generation, @[CBH 94|5PFH 3e Compendium Includes Bug Hunt#94])
- Set up Points of interest and Salvage (Salvage mission only)

**Post-battle activities** (step 8)
- a. Resolve Rival status (@[p.119|Hostile setting#119])
- b. Check for illegal Psionic usage (@[p.21|Hostile setting#21])
- c. Resolve Patron status (@[p.119|Hostile setting#119])
- d. Roll quest progress (@[p.120|Hostile setting#120], @[CBH p.78|5PFH 3e Compendium Includes Bug Hunt#78])
- e. Get paid (@[p.120|Hostile setting#120])
- f. Trade Salvage (@[p.147|Hostile setting#147] — Salvage mission only)
- g. Battlefield finds (@[p.121|Hostile setting#121])
- h. Check for Salvage mission points of interest (@[CBH p.139|5PFH 3e Compendium Includes Bug Hunt#139] — Salvage mission only)
- i. Check for World Event Steps
- j. Check for Settlement Event Steps
- k. Gather the loot (@[p.121|Hostile setting#121], @[p.131+|Hostile setting#131])
- l. Determine injuries and recovery (@[p.121|Hostile setting#121])
- m. EXP and character upgrades (@[p.123|Hostile setting#123])
- n. Invest in advanced training (@[p.124|Hostile setting#124])
- o. Purchase items (@[p.125|Hostile setting#125])
- p. Roll for a character event (@[p.126|Hostile setting#126], @[p.128|Hostile setting#128])
- q. Resolve Heat check

**Travel Steps**
- Flee invasion (@[Core p.69|5PFH Five Parsecs From Home v3#69]).
- Check for Factions fleeing (@[CBH p.114|5PFH 3e Compendium Includes Bug Hunt#114]).
- Decide whether to travel (@[Core p.69|5PFH Five Parsecs From Home v3#69]).
- Resolve steps, Local Travel / Starship Travel (@[Core p.70|5PFH Five Parsecs From Home v3#70]), as applicable.
- Resolve New World Arrival steps (@[Core p.72|5PFH Five Parsecs From Home v3#72])
- Check for Shipping Issues (Starship, Planetary, Overland).
- Check for Settlement Event Steps.

**Settlement Event Steps**
- Check for invasion (@[Core p.69|5PFH Five Parsecs From Home v3#69]).
- Check for Instability (@[CBH p.148|5PFH 3e Compendium Includes Bug Hunt#148]).
- Roll a Negotiations check.
- Check for Faction Conflict
- Check for Psionic legality status (@[p.20|Hostile setting#20])
- Resolve Heat Check.

**World Event Steps**
- Check for Faction Conflict.
- Fringe World Strife / Instability
- Roll for a Campaign Event (@[Core p.126|5PFH Five Parsecs From Home v3#126]).
- Roll for a District Event.
- Roll for a Faction Event (@[CBH p.114|5PFH 3e Compendium Includes Bug Hunt#114]).
- Check for Galactic War progress (@[Core p.126|5PFH Five Parsecs From Home v3#126]).
- Resolve Heat Check.

END OF CAMPAIGN TURN
